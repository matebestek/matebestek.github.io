01 — About

Driven by curiosity — an explorer of reality with a strong sense for people, technology and the life sciences, and the ability to listen, structure, innovate, and finish what was started.

A highly creative person who enjoys connections in life — between people or concepts — that can be very diverse, thanks to an inherent interdisciplinarity. My work sits where healthcare, technology, policy and human behaviour meet.

I also love to travel and grow more sensitive to different cultures. My close family communicates in five languages — together with a love for music and art, this cultural diversity is a big part of who I am.

03 — Focus

What I work on

Healthcare interoperability

Designing systems that speak to one another — IHE, openEHR, HL7, SNOMED and Continua at national scale.

AI in cancer screening

Machine learning, synthetic data, LLM/RAG systems and clinical agents applied to real screening programmes.

National eHealth & governance

Architecture, standards, and governance of common eHealth artefacts, databases and services on the national level.

Cross-border health data

Patient-summary exchange across EU member states — epSOS, CEF and eIDAS-aligned national contact points.

Health data semantics

Doctoral research on democratizing and commoning semantic interoperability for sustainable health systems.

Socio-technical leadership

Managing change, risk and stakeholders on high-stakes healthcare programmes, with teams of up to 60 people.
